### 1. Midnight Boot Polish

![Midnight Boot Polish](/_images/0/0c05b4103347f2ec5b759a5e3ee4f314-medium.webp)

Cowboys believed polishing boots at midnight brought luck. They did it in secret groups. Rumors said it warded off ghosts. Some refused to do it fearing bad omens.

### 2. Coffee Ground Fortunes

![Coffee Ground Fortunes](/_images/7/76486388e0c1703aa3605a78b277cf96-medium.webp)

Reading coffee grounds predicted the day's dangers. Groups gathered around the pot each morning. Funny predictions often came true by chance. Some cowboys avoided strong brews to skip bad news.

### 3. Rattlesnake Boot Dance

![Rattlesnake Boot Dance](/_images/1/14da35c1f6ad2607c27dbbd64dfa126f-medium.webp)

Dancing in boots made from snakeskin warded off bites. Performed around campfires at night. Rumors claimed it scared away predators. Participants looked ridiculous but felt safer.

### 4. Spur Jingle Codes

![Spur Jingle Codes](/_images/3/3478f426398fba3c22263f970e7db329-medium.webp)

Spurs had patterns to send messages via jingles. Used during cattle drives for warnings. Contradictory stories exist about its effectiveness. Outlaws supposedly cracked the codes easily.

### 5. Horse Hair Braids

![Horse Hair Braids](/_images/3/34a884565ce4e687aebf28eec92837c5-medium.webp)

Braiding horse hair into bracelets brought good fortune. Done only with shed hair not cut. Scary tales warned of curses if done wrong. Many kept them hidden under sleeves.

### 6. Dust Devil Wishes

![Dust Devil Wishes](/_images/1/1a0e27629f88d2fd31ded70d7817aa38-medium.webp)

Chasing dust devils to make a wish was common. They ran in circles yelling requests. Contradictory results led to arguments. Some cowboys got hurt trying too hard.

### 7. Bandana Knot Secrets

![Bandana Knot Secrets](/_images/7/7fcc61cb9958b2b4831c6094db82eb19-medium.webp)

Tying bandanas in specific knots hid messages. Used for love notes or alerts. Funny mixups happened often in groups. This practice faded with the telegraph.
