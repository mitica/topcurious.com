### 1. Skyscrapers Beside Temples

![Skyscrapers Beside Temples](/_images/a/a4e08457569bd9933929470f252ca9d0-medium.webp)

Modern towers dominate city skylines. Ancient temples remain tucked between them. Locals burn incense at dawn. They rush to tech jobs by noon.

### 2. Luxury Bags With Cash Payments

![Luxury Bags With Cash Payments](/_images/0/0e0744cc739a724024f24640b267913b-medium.webp)

People carry expensive Western brands. Cash is still preferred over cards. Flashy displays mix with thrift habits. Social status shifts with each purchase.

### 3. One Child Policy Echoes

![One Child Policy Echoes](/_images/8/8a69554379fa77402b527aad5356eda9-medium.webp)

Families still center around one child. Grandparents share tiny apartments. Spoiling coexists with strict discipline. Future support burdens grow heavier.

### 4. Censored Internet With Viral Trends

![Censored Internet With Viral Trends](/_images/7/7c98810c067b0eda03b4bf5f9444dfb1-medium.webp)

Platforms block many global sites. Yet local apps spread memes fast. Users find creative workarounds daily. Trends explode despite heavy monitoring.

### 5. Green Tech Next To Pollution

![Green Tech Next To Pollution](/_images/a/a3e018263f45931e90d0c4a81716b179-medium.webp)

China leads in solar panel output. Coal plants still operate nearby. Electric vehicles fill new roads. Air quality alerts continue in winter.

### 6. Traditional Medicine In Hospitals

![Traditional Medicine In Hospitals](/_images/7/72b240bbbc7617023a22b0b027d7cb49-medium.webp)

Western medicine fills modern hospitals. Herbal remedies are prescribed alongside. Patients often try both treatments. Doctors blend old formulas with scans.
